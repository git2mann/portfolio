Some Of Ink: A Fresh Perspective on Old Tracks

Published on April 10, 2025 | By Leon Nduati

Working on Some Of Ink was an incredible journey. This EP gave me the opportunity to revisit tracks from my 2021 release, Son Of Ink, and breathe new life into them. It was a chance to reflect on my growth as an artist and approach these songs with a fresh lens and perspective.

The process was both challenging and rewarding. Revisiting old tracks felt like reconnecting with an old friend—familiar yet different. I wanted to honor the original essence of these songs while exploring new creative directions. It was about finding the balance between nostalgia and innovation.

Why "Some Of Ink"?

The name Some Of Ink reflects the idea of revisiting a part of my past work. It’s not a complete reimagining but rather a curated selection of tracks that resonated deeply with me. Each song was chosen for its potential to evolve and tell a new story.

What to Expect

Some Of Ink is a blend of the old and the new. Fans of Son Of Ink will recognize the core elements of the original tracks, but they’ll also notice fresh twists and surprises. From updated lyrics to remastered instrumentals, this EP truly felt like a celebration of evolution and creativity, especially after such a long period of silence.

I’m incredibly proud of how this project turned out, and I can’t wait for you to hear it. Whether you’re a longtime fan or new to my music, I hope Some Of Ink resonates with you and offers a glimpse into my journey as an artist.

Thank you for joining me on this journey. I look forward to sharing more music and stories with you in the future.

— Leon Nduati
(You may know him as Klense).
